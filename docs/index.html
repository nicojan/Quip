What it does

Everything between you and the right GIF, removed.

Skip the browser tab, the saved file, and the drag across your screen. Quip keeps the search, your favorites, and the GIFs you already used one click from wherever you're typing.

Search and copy

Search Giphy, click, and it's copied

Type a search in the menu bar and click any result to copy it as an animated file. Then paste it wherever you'd paste an image. No download, no drag and drop.

Favorites and recently copied

The one you reach for twice a day, always there

Star a GIF to keep it one click away. Quip also remembers the GIFs you actually used, so your go-to reactions sit at the top of the popover, waiting.

Collections

Group your favorites into named buckets

Sort your starred GIFs into collections. Give each one an emoji, drag to reorder them, and sort them A to Z. The reaction folder you always wanted.

Three sizes

As wide as you want, no jumping around

Switch between two, three, and five GIFs per row. Every size is the same height, so only the width changes and the popover stays put.

One-time setup

Bring your own Giphy key

Quip ships without an API key. You add your own free key from Giphy, which takes about a minute. The upside: you're never behind someone else's rate limit, and the key never leaves your Mac.

  1. Open the Giphy developers dashboard

    Go to the Giphy developers dashboard and sign in, or make a free account.

  2. Create an app and pick the API option

    Giphy nudges you toward the SDK, but Quip needs a plain API key. Choose API, then copy the key it gives you.

  3. Paste the key into Quip's Settings

    Open Quip's Settings and paste the key into the Giphy API Key field. That's the whole setup.

Why a key of your own?

Sharing one key across every user means sharing one rate limit. Give Quip your own free key and your searches never wait behind anyone else's.

Until you add a key, Quip's search asks you for one.

Quip stores the key on your Mac and has no server of its own to send it to.
Questions

Good to know

Is Quip free?

Yes. Quip is free and open source under the MIT license. No trial, no subscription.

Why do I need my own Giphy key?

So you never share a rate limit with other Quip users. It's free to get, and it lives only on your Mac.

Which version of macOS does Quip need?

macOS 14.6 or later.

How does Quip update itself?

Through Sparkle, built in. Quip checks for new versions and installs them, and every build is signed and notarized.

Is Quip on the Mac App Store?

No. Quip is a direct download: a signed, notarized app that updates itself. You install it from the download rather than the App Store.

Does Quip track my searches or GIFs?

No. Quip talks to Giphy from your Mac with your own key. There is no Quip server and no tracking.
